Why HouseCupAThon

Once upon a time, a magical world of witches and wizards was born

  • June 1997

    Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

    When J.K Rowling published her first book she set the world on fire! Everyone had a copy and the craze for the series wasn't going anywhere. I remember my teacher reading it to myself, and her other students, and I have to admit, it really got kids excited about reading!

  • November 2001

    An Epic Movie Franchise is born

    By the time the first movie came out in 2001, I think it's safe to say that everyone knew about the Harry Potter story. The Warner Bros bought the rights to the book in 1999 for a reported $1.6 million and the movie was a huge success grossing $974.8 million at the box office worldwide.

  • May 2007

    The Wizarding World of Harry Potter Announced

    An astounding 10 years after the release of the first novel 'Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone' Universal, in partnership with Warner Bros., officially announced The Wizarding World of Harry Potter, a Harry Potter theme park (currently with locations in Florida, California, and Japan)

  • January 2019

    HouseCupAThon is BORN!

    I wanted to create a readathon that got everyone together talking about the things we love most, books. I thought what a better way to do that than by celebrating the series that made so many around the world, including myself, fall in love with reading! I am so happy you are here, and I hope you join us for this readathon!
